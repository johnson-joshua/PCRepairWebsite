ABOUT US

Quick Computer Repair is owned and operated by Joshua Johnson. Josh has been repairing computers for friends and family for many years, and loves to be able to help other people with something that they can't do themselves. Josh is CompTIA A+ and Network+ certified, so you can rest assured that your computer and the data it contains will be safe in his hands. Most jobs can be completed within 24 hours, but there are some exceptions. You can either bring your computer to him or Josh will come to your house or business to examine the problems that you are having with your computer.
